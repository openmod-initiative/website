About

We are a grass root initiative of modellers from various universities and research institutes across Europe.

Aim

Open models and open data will advance knowledge and lead to better energy policies. Open up energy models improves quality, transparency, and credibility, leading to better research and policy advice.

View manifesto »

Activities

The openmod initiative is a network, an interest group, and a platform. We exchange ideas and source code, lobby for policy support for open projects, and actively share data, code and knowhow.
